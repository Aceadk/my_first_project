## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Active Accounts

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service:

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Profile Information | Duration of account |
| Messages | Duration of account |
| Photos/Media | Duration of account |
| Usage Data | 24 months (rolling) |
| Analytics Data | Aggregated indefinitely |

### 5.2 After Account Deletion

When you delete your account, we process your data as follows:

**Immediately Removed:**
- Profile visibility (hidden from other users)
- Discovery feed presence
- Match accessibility

**14-Day Recovery Period:**
- Account data preserved (allows reactivation)
- Data inaccessible to other users

**After 14 Days:**
- Profile data permanently deleted
- Photos and media deleted
- Matches and conversations deleted

**Retained for Limited Period:**
| Data Type | Retention | Reason |
|-----------|-----------|--------|
| Transaction Records | 7 years | Tax and legal compliance |
| Communication Logs | 1 year | Legal compliance |
| Safety Reports | 3 years | Trust & Safety |
| Legal Hold Data | As required | Ongoing legal matters |

**Permanently Retained:**
| Data Type | Format | Reason |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| Aggregated Analytics | Anonymized | Product improvement |
| Statistical Data | De-identified | Research |

### 5.3 Deactivated Accounts

If you deactivate (not delete) your account:

- Data preserved for up to 6 months
- Profile hidden from other users
- Can reactivate at any time during this period
- After 6 months, account may be deleted

### 5.4 Legal Requirements

We may retain data longer when required by:

- Applicable law or regulation
- Court order or legal process
- Ongoing investigation or dispute
- Regulatory audit or examination

### 5.5 Backup Systems

Deleted data may persist in backup systems for a reasonable period (typically 30 days). This data:

- Is not accessible for normal operations
- Is not used for any purpose
- Is permanently purged during routine backup rotation

## 6. Your Privacy Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access Your Data

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you:

**Self-Service Access:**
- View your profile information in the app
- Review your privacy settings
- View your subscription and payment history

**Data Export:**
- Request a copy of your data: Settings > Privacy > Export Data
- Receive data in JSON format
- Processing time: Up to 30 days

### 6.2 Correct Your Data

You can update or correct your information:

**Self-Service Corrections:**
- Edit your profile at any time
- Update your preferences and settings
- Change your email or phone number (verification required)

**Limitations:**
- Name changes: Once every 30 days
- Date of birth: Once every 30 days
- Some data may require support assistance

### 6.3 Delete Your Data

You can delete your account and associated data:

**Self-Service Deletion:**
1. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
2. Confirm your decision
3. 14-day recovery period begins
4. After 14 days, data is permanently deleted

**What Gets Deleted:**
- Profile and photos
- Matches and conversations
- Preferences and settings
- Most personal data

**What May Be Retained:**
- Data required for legal compliance
- Anonymized/aggregated analytics
- Safety-related records

### 6.4 Data Portability

You can receive your data in a portable format:

- Request via Settings > Privacy > Export Data
- Receive data in structured JSON format
- Includes profile, preferences, and history
- Does not include other users' data

### 6.5 Restrict Processing

In certain circumstances, you can request that we restrict how we process your data:

- While we verify correction requests
- If processing is unlawful but you don't want deletion
- If we no longer need the data but you need it for legal claims
- While we evaluate objection requests

### 6.6 Object to Processing

You can object to certain types of processing:

- Direct marketing (always honored)
- Profiling for marketing purposes
- Processing based on legitimate interests (case-by-case evaluation)

### 6.7 Withdraw Consent

Where we rely on consent for processing, you can withdraw consent at any time:

- This does not affect processing before withdrawal
- May impact service functionality
- Can be done through app settings or by contacting us

### 6.8 Opt-Out Options

**Push Notifications:**
- In-App: Settings > Notifications
- Device Settings: iOS/Android notification settings
- Granular control over notification types

**Email Communications:**
- Unsubscribe link in all marketing emails
- In-App: Settings > Notifications > Email

**Location Tracking:**
- Device Settings: Disable location permissions
- In-App: Settings > Privacy > Location

**Analytics:**

- Note: Basic usage data may still be collected

**Personalized Ads:**

### 6.9 Exercising Your Rights

**Self-Service:**
Most rights can be exercised through app settings.

**Contact Us:**
For requests requiring assistance:
- Email: privacy@crush.app
- Subject: Privacy Rights Request
- Include: Account email, specific request, verification info

**Response Time:**
