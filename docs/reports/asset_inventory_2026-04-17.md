# Asset Inventory — 2026-04-17

Scope:
- `assets/**`
- `android/app/src/main/res/**`
- `ios/Runner/Assets.xcassets/**`

Goal:
- identify which assets are runtime-required,
- identify which files are tooling-only or platform-native inputs,
- remove bundle-only baggage from Flutter asset declarations without deleting source art used by icon/native generation.
